id: mindgame
term: Mindgame
tags: [strategy]
links: [mixup]
---

A situation where two opposing players have discrete right and wrong options, and each attempts to manipulate the other into making a mistake. A common example of a mindgame involves a specific interaction (engine) between two abilities that counter each other, such as Sion’s Q versus Fiora’s W, where each player wants to use their ability to negate the other’s. Mindgames primarily focus on prediction and guesswork rather than reaction-based execution.
