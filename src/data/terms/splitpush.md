---
id: splitpush
term: Splitpush
tags: [strategy]
alternates: ["splitting"]
---

Pushing a sidelane while split from the team. A splitpush puts pressure on the opposing team to consider sending one or more people to answer the splitpusher, giving the splitpusher’s allies time to make a play on the opposite side of the map. Splitpushing, an isolated play is often contrasted with teamfighting, a grouped play.
