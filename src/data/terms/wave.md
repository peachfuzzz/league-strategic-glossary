---
id: wave
term: Wave
tags: [game-mechanics, minions]
alternates: ["minion wave"]
---

A group of minions. A wave can refer to a specific group of minions spawned by either nexus on a regular interval or any arbitrary collection of minions in a lane. Minion waves spawn at 30-second intervals (decreasing to 25, then 20 seconds with game time as of Season 2026), and are also used as a measurement of time.
