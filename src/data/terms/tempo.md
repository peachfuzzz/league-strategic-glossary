---
id: tempo
term: Tempo
tags: [abstract-concepts]
---

The efficiency of spent time in comparison to the opponent. In League, time on the map always competes with the collection of risk-free gold and experience—minion waves for laners and jungle camps for junglers. Tempo can be measured as the time to act without incurring a significant opportunity cost, often compared to this baseline value. For example, a player might be considered “behind tempo” if they have significant standing resources yet to collect while their counterpart has already finished.
