---
id: proxy
term: Proxy
tags: [strategy, minions]
---

Farming the enemy minion waves between standing enemy turrets. Proxying minimizes lane interactions and creates a tempo advantage for the proxying player at the cost of increased risk of death upon an enemy collapse. Since the enemy minion waves are killed before they can meet with the allied wave, the allied wave will always crash without interruption unless the opposing laner interferes, while also preventing the opposing laner from pushing with their own wave to threaten tower damage. In certain matchups, proxying can force the lower waveclear laner to remain under their tower while the proxying player can farm uncontested.
