---
id: high-elo
term: High Elo
tags: [vernacular]
---

The ladder ratings where players are considered very skilled at the game. The exact threshold of high elo is a contentious topic among the community, with definitions of “high elo” ranging from Emerald+ to 1k LP Challenger. Riot’s balance framework, as of 2020, defines Elite play as Diamond 2 and onwards.
