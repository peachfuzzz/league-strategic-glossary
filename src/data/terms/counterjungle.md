---
id: counterjungle
term: Counterjungle
tags: [strategy, jungle]
---

Invading the opposing team’s jungle to kill their jungle camps. Counterjungling is a common response taken when the enemy jungler shows on the map, often considered a crossmap play. Unlike clearing allied camps, counterjungling’s effects are twofold: the player gains the gold and experience from killing the camp, while the opposing jungler loses access to the same resources, creating a net relative advantage of two camps.
