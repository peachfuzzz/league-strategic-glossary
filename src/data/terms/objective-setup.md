---
id: objective-setup
term: Objective Setup
tags: [strategy]
---

The sequence of preparing a play around a neutral objective. The term objective setup can be used in specific, referring to a prepared, high-coordination sequence a team performs before an objective, or in general, referring to the general situation before a neutral objective is contested. The goal of an objective setup is to make the following contest easier, through a mixture of wave management, map movement, vision control, and synchronizing tempo.
