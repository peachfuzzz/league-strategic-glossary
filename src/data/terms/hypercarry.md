---
id: hypercarry
term: Hypercarry
tags: [vernacular]
---

A champion with a kit that scales particularly well into the late game. The specific attributes that determine a hypercarry are up for debate, but generally hypercarries have high, reliable, target-agnostic damage with lots of multiplicative scalings. Marksmen make up the majority of hypercarry candidates, as basic attacks are very reliable and already have several multiplicative scalers in attack speed, attack damage, and crit chance.
