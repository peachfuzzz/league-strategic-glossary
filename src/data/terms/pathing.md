---
id: pathing
term: Pathing
tags: [strategy, jungle]
alternates: ["jungle pathing"]
---

The route a jungler takes on the map. Jungle pathing also encompasses the skill of planning such a route and the decision-making process of deciding where to go and when. “Pathing toward” is sometimes used in a similar context as the prioritization of the object that a jungler paths towards, e.g. “pathing towards bot.”
