---
id: spike
term: Spike
tags: [strategy]
alternates: ["powerspike"]
---

A significant increase in power level. The significance of a spike is not strictly defined, though generally spikes refer to power increases that unlock new capabilities for a champion. Spikes that are commonly discussed include level 2, level 3, level 6, and completed items. Skilled players often formulate their gameplans around spikes, since they are a clear reference point to power and can be used to surprise opponents.
